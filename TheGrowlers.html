The Growlers

The Growlers is an American Band formed in Dana Point, California. The Growlers sound is described as a trademark style that combines surf, pop, rock and beat. It has been labeled as Beach Goth.

Visit The Growlers The Growlers were signed with Everloving Records in 2009. It was under Everloving Records that they released five studio albums:

  • Are You In or Out (2009)
  • Hot Tropics (2010)
  • Gilded Pleasures (2013)
  • Hung at Heart (2013)
  • Chinese Foundation (2014)
In 2014 after their release of Chinese Foundation they mutually agreed to part ways with Everloving Records. In 2016 The Growlers signed with Cult Records and relased their sixth studio album, City Club (2016). After this The Growlers decided to create their own label called Beach Goth Records. In 2018 they announced they were planning to release their seventh studio album that is set to release in 2019 called Casual Acquaintances.

The Growlers Members

  • Singer: Brooks Nielsen
  • Lead Guitar: Matt Taylor
  • Keyboard: Kyle Straka

Hot Tropics
Hot Tropics is The Growlers second studio album and it was released uder Everloving Records. When it comes to sound and production Hot Tropics is by far my favorite album. Although Chinese Foundation introduced me to The Growlers Hot Tropics was the album that introduced me to what Beach Goth entails. To me it was the standard and introduction to what I should expect from The Growlers in future projects.
City Club
