Why Did I Choose NIU?

Northern Illinois University offered me an opportunity to pursue a degree in Operations Management and Information Systems. When talking to the advisors, students, and professors they even began talking about Data Analytics which was a path that other schools were behind in offering.

I remember Jordan Lynch's run with the NIU Football team and his candidacy for Heisman. After that year there was only one school in Illinois that I could support. I grew attached to the underdog. Many of the students here often feel like they are overlooked. It is common to graduate here with a chip on your shoulder, or with something to prove. And that is the mentality and mindsite that I have developed as well.
