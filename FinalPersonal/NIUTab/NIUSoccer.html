NIU Men's Soccer Club

My Freshman year I tryed out for the Northern Illinois University Men's Soccer Club. At the time it was a small program of about 15 guys that played recreationally and played friendlies against teams like Iowa University. A year later I wanted to help the club grow so I ran for Treasurer. I was voted as the treasurer for the club and I helped the team develop and ultimately join an elite conference known as the Midwest Alliance Soccer Conference. I am now the president of the club and will continue as president into my Senior year.
