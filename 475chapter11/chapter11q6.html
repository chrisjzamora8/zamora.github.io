The Red Balloon Project - Skizzy Mars

April 11, 2019


The Red Balloon Project is the first extended playlist (EP) released by Skizzy Mars in 2015. It was in preparation for his first studio album "Alone Together." This EP brought a new era of Skizzy Mars with an improvement of production and lyrics. The Red Balloon Project is not the best album of all time. However, I find this EP to be very nostalgic. It was the first piece that introduced me to one of my favorite genres of music, Alternative Rap. This EP brings me back to my high school days where I would throw headphones on as I walked from class to class, or as I waited patiently under the lights on our turf field to prepare for every match. It brings me back to a time in my youth where I was learning who Christopher Zamora was, and I have The Red Balloon Project and Skizzy Mars to thank for that. Also, this album is just dynamic. You could listen to it while you where at a bonfire with your friends, driving around late at night with "just a friend," even studying.
