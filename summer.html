Door County Summer

Wisconsin's Door County Peninsula is ecologically diverse — upland and boreal forest, bogs, swamps, sand and rock beaches, limestone escarpments, and farmlands. The variety of ecosystems supports a large number of wildflower species.

Explore Door County During The Summer

Yellow Lady’s Slipper facts: 4–24 inches tall; perennial; native plant; grows in wet, shaded deciduous woods, swamps, and bogs; an orchid; official flower of Door County
